1. 2 dices per player 
2. 5 Rounds, Bonus points for doubles 
3. If a player gets an even number then their answer is +10 
4. If a player gets an odd number on both dices their answer is -5
5. Once all 5 rounds are done all the rolls of each player from the previous rounds are added to make a total score 
and the one with the most total score wins
